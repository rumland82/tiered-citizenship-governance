# Taxation & Economy

## Sales Tax
- Flat 35% on all goods, property, and rent
- Imports taxed +5%
- Exports untaxed

---

## Corporate Tax
- Income tax on corporations only
- Funds education system
- Businesses receive citizenship vouchers

---

## Substance Tax
- Alcohol, tobacco, drugs legalized
- Additional 15% tax
- Medical needs covered by healthcare system
