# Rights & Legal Protections

## Civil Rights
- Freedom of speech (except incitement to action)
- Freedom of association
- Right to privacy

---

## Right to Bear Arms
- Regulated ownership
- Background and mental health checks
- Mandatory training
- Proper storage required
- Tiered access to higher-grade arms
- All weapons registered
- Ballistics stored separately from owner identity
- Owner identity requires court order
- Rights revocable through due process

---

## Property Rights
- Right to own property
- Baseline housing is residency-only
- No seizure except death with no next of kin

---

## Due Process
- Equality under the law
- Presumption of innocence
- Right to legal representation
- Judges removable by vote
