# Voting & Representation

## Voting Eligibility
- Begins at Tier 2 Citizenship

---

## Voting Scope
- City, county, state, and federal
- One vote per citizen per jurisdiction

---

## Property-Based Voting
- Property ownership grants voting rights regardless of residency
- Corporate-owned property grants no vote unless owned by T2C+

---

## Vote Timing
- Laws proposed within first 10 months of year
- Votes occur in final 2 months only
