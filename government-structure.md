# Government Structure

## Branches
- Infrastructure
- Humanities & Human Resources
- Transportation
- Enforcement & Defense
- Judicial
- Oversight
- Financing

Branches operate independently and interact only when required.

---

## Leadership
- Appointed by branch councils
- Councils removable by vote
- Tier requirements apply to leadership level

---

## Oversight
- Oversees all government branches
- No authority over private citizens
- Oversight leadership removable by public vote
